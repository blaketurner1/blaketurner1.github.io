About This Project

This was the final project for Design 3. For this project we were tasked with redesigning a Vinyl LP. I chose the album, "In the Court of the Crimson King" by King Crimson. This progressive rock album was released in 1969 - at the time one of the only of its kind. Some would say that King Crimson pioneered this genre, and after listening to it many times over the course of this project, I would have to agree.

Matching the themes and mood of the album was tricky at first, but with some work I was able to capture the essence of the album. In the Court of the Crimson King is about man's relationship with society, and how we fit into it. The mood is very dark and serious at times, but whimsical at others. To capture that feeling I used a deep and vibrant color pallete, along with original artwork for the backgrounds.
